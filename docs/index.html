Senzing Free Support

Senzing offers free support for paid, complimentary, and trial licenses.

Community forums

Senzing community discussions are located at https://senzing.community In the discussions you can ask how to integrate SenzingAPI and Senzing Community resources into your existing infrastructure.

GitHub issues

To request features or to report bugs in individual repositories, visit the repository of interest in the Senzing community, click on "issues", then click on "New issue" to open a new request.

Email

If you want to keep private information out of public discussion groups, feel free to send email to support@senzing.com.
